Privacy Policy for ForexFinder

Last Updated:

ForexFinder ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our service to locate currency exchange shops.

1. Information We Collect

A. User Information

When you sign in to ForexFinder, we collect the following information via your Google account to authenticate you and personalize your experience:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Google Profile Picture

We do not store sensitive personal information in our authentication tokens (JWT). All data transmission is secured using HTTPS and TLS encryption.

B. Location Data

To provide our core service—finding nearby currency exchange shops—we request access to your device's real-time location. This data is used solely to display relevant shops and rates in your vicinity via Google Maps integration.

C. Shop Information (For Shop Owners)

If you are a shop owner listing your business, we collect and display:

  • Shop Name
  • Physical Location
  • Contact Information
  • Operating Hours
  • Currency Exchange Rates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To provide location-based services, such as showing nearby exchange shops on a map.
  • To allow shop owners to manage their listings and advertise rates.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the App.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement the following measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS with a Certbot TLS certificate.
  • Authentication: We use JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure user authentication. Your personal information is not embedded within these tokens.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with third-party services, specifically:

  • Google Maps API: To provide map interfaces and location services. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication purposes.
